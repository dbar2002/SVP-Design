Community Outreach

SVP Reach Back

Technical training and community outreach — diversifying the technology industry one student at a time.

SVP's goal is to address the lack of Black and African American representation in Silicon Valley and the technology industry. With dedicated technology partners, we're building a training pipeline that major corporations can adopt for years to come.

Location

San Francisco / Oakland

Bay Area headquarters and primary training hub.

Location

New Orleans / Slidell

Gulf Coast expansion serving the greater Louisiana area.

Location

Atlanta Metropolitan

Southeast hub tapping into Atlanta's growing tech scene.

Program Structure

Student Selection & Funding

Starting with 30 students per metro area, with a goal of scaling to 100+ as the program grows.

Life Coaching & Mentorship

Connecting students with life coaches, family counselors, health professionals, and mentors committed to long-term support.

Finance & Credit 101

Before tech training begins, students learn basic finance, money management, credit, and saving — partnering with community banks.

Microsoft Technical Training & Certification

Industry-recognized certifications preparing students for employment with Bay Area tech companies and beyond.
